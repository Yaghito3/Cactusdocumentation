# Cosmetics

Cactus Mod has its own set of (more or less) unique cosmetics - some with an actual meaning and some that exist without any explicit reason. This page covers what they are, which (relevant ones) exist and how to obtain them.

## Relevant Cosmetics
These are relevant cosmetics (currently only capes are shown here) which are linked to a specific circumstance and can / could be obtained by all players.

### Milestones
Milestone capes like the **1000 Member** and **1 Year Anniversary** Cape are awarded to all players who have been around before / at the time the respective milestone was reached.

![Settings](/static/cosmetics/1000_member.webp)
![Settings](/static/cosmetics/one_year.webp)

> The **1000 Member** and **1 Year Anniversary** Capes can no longer be obtained.

### Events
Event capes like the **Place 2024**, **Gamescom 2024** and **Place 2025** Cape are awarded to all players who have participated in the respective Event. Conditions and the definition of "participating" can vary and depends on the circumstances of the Event.

![Settings](/static/cosmetics/place2024.webp)
![Settings](/static/cosmetics/gamescom2024.webp)
![Settings](/static/cosmetics/place2025.webp)

> The **Place 2024**, **Gamescom 2024** and **Place 2025** Capes can no longer be obtained.

### Time Based
Time Based capes like the **Christmas 2024** and **April Fools 2025 (Clippy)** Cape are awarded to all players who have played during a specific timespan.

![Settings](/static/cosmetics/christmas_2024.webp)
![Settings](/static/cosmetics/clippy.webp)

> The **Christmas 2024** and **April Fools 2025 (Clippy)** Capes can no longer be obtained.

## Obtaining Cosmetics
There are many ways to obtain cosmetics. Here are (almost) all of them.

### 🥇 Milestones
For some milestones (like the 1000 members milestone), every registered user receives a cape.
As long as you have logged in before / during that event, you should obtain the cape automatically.

### 🚪 Playing during Events
For some other events like the 1st anniversary, every user playing during that event receives a cape.
As long as you play cactus once in that timespan, you should obtain the cape automatically.

### 🛠️ Assigned by the Team
Staff members can manually assign capes to users. These are mostly random capes we've created for fun - feel free to ask for one (but please don't permanently nag us about it!)

Some capes like the developer cape are only given to team members.

### 🎫 Single-use codes
For some events like the Gamescom and for some special capes, single-use codes are given out to some users.
They can be redeemed using `/redeem <code> <username>` in our [Discord Server](https://cactusmod.xyz/go?discord)

> There will me more/other ways to obtain capes in future, and more Events for you to participate in!
